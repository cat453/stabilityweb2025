The TVL HE

The Technical-Vocational-Livelihood (TVL) track in Senior High School (SHS) includes TVL Home Economics (HE), a strand that focuses on livelihood skills for jobs in the food, fashion, tourist, and hospitality industries.

Food and beverage, hospitality, fashion, and tourism are just a few of the industries for which it seeks to provide students with employable skills.

Students gain knowledge in subjects like baking, cooking, bartending, tourism, housekeeping, handicrafts, and clothing design.
